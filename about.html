I am a PhD student in behavioural ecology working under the supervision of Daniel Redhead, Richard McElreath, Julia Ostner and Oliver Schülke. I am currently based in Vienna, where I work as a Resident Fellow at the Konrad Lorenz Institute, in Vienna, and am otherwise affiliated with the University of Göttingen, the German Primate Center, and the Max Planck Institute for Evolutionary Anthropology.
I am broadly interested in building bridges between theory and data to explain variation in animal behaviour. In my PhD research, I am (trying to) build a causal framework for the drivers of animal social network structure, using probabilistic and causal modelling.
I also have a personal “blog” here, where I simply list things — films, novels, anything — that affected me.
